-MY BELOVED FRIENDS-

On this part, I want to share about all the close friends who have been a part of my life. I’ve listed my circle of friends from secondary school as well as those I met at university.🤎

My secondary school friends are mostly people I’ve known since 2017, when we were in Form 1. However, some of them I had already known since primary school, and they happened to go to the same secondary school as me, so our friendship continued to grow. Most of my circle back then consisted of students who stayed in the hostel. It was such a great time because all my secondary school friends were kind, helpful, and absolutely hilarious.Even though it’s harder to meet up now since everyone is busy with their own path,some studying at different universities and with mismatched holidays but it doesn’t change the fact that the memories we created during school are still fresh in my mind.

Next, my friends at university consist of my classmates and, most importantly, my roommates. It’s really fun being friends with them because we’re all on the same wavelength. Especially my roommates,they’re incredibly understanding and caring toward me.Being friends with them has also given me a lot of new knowledge about their home states, as most of my university friends come from different parts of the country. It’s been such a valuable experience for me!
